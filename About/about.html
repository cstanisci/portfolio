ABOUT ME

Full Stack Developer and former financial analyst passionate about creating useful applications and user-friendly programs through efficient code. I leverage my skills in analysis, problem-solving, and team-building to inspire those around me and to promote a meritocratic and solutions-oriented workplace environment. I thrive on creating well-crafted, data-driven solutions to complex problems, with a special interest in film, wellness, and hospitality.

I am insatiably curious, I crave intellectual challenge, and I thrive in environments that foster that drive. I graduated Cum Laude from Georgetown University in Washington, D.C. with a B.S. in Finance and a minor in Theology. Prior to embarking down the developer path, I worked in business development for Yelp, in financial analysis for The Stilwell Group, an activist hedge fund, and, currently, in corporate finance and strategy for Cater2.me, a hospitality startup.

Based in Denver now, I'm a Jersey boy with a Colorado soul. When I'm not coding, you can find me in the mountains hiking and skiing, or on the go, exploring new countries, cities, and yet-to-be-discovered corners of the Earth.
